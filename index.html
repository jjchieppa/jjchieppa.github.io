My Research

Southern Pine Decline and Ecosystem Vulnerability

My work on southern pine decline investigated the physiological responses of trees to root-infecting fungi and environmental stressors, such as drought and soil nutrient depletion. Through a combination of field experiments and laboratory studies, I examined the interactive effects of biotic and abiotic stressors on root function, water transport, and overall tree health. My research aimed to identify early indicators of decline and improve forecasting models for ecosystem vulnerability under climate change.
