Biography

I am a research scientist passionate about plant ecology, climate, and the intersection of data science with biological systems.

My background includes work in plant physiology, ecophysiology, and crop protection, leveraging machine learning and AI to enhance agricultural forecasting.

From 2022 to 2025, I was part of a machine learning team focused on predicting disease outcomes and optimizing treatment strategies for crops. Before that, I explored the physiological responses of plants to environmental stressors, including drought, ozone exposure, and soil pathogens.

Now, I am eager to apply my data science toolbox back into the world of ecology and environmental research, integrating large-scale datasets with predictive modeling to tackle pressing challenges in conservation and agriculture.
